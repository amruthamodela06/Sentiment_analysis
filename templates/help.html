Frequently Asked Questions and Guide

How to use Sentra?

Simply type or paste text into the main input box on the home page. Click "Analyze" to see an immediate emotional assessment.

What do the results mean?

Sentra categorizes text into moods like "Stress", "Anxiety", or "Normal".

  • Primary Signal: The strongest detected emotion.
  • Confidence: How sure the AI is about this result.

Is this medical advice?

No. Sentra is an AI tool for self-reflection only. It cannot diagnose mental health conditions. If you are in crisis, please use the Resources page.
