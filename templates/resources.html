Support Resources

If you are feeling overwhelmed, support is available. You are not alone.

Emergency Contacts

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (USA): Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International: Find a Helpline

Self-Care Tips

  • Reach out to a trusted friend or family member.
  • Engage in grounding exercises (e.g., deep breathing).
  • Step away from screens and take a walk.
