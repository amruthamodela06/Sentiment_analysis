About Sentra

Sentra is an AI-powered sentiment analysis tool designed to help you understand emotions in text.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, non-judgmental space for self-reflection.

How it Works

We use machine learning (Natural Language Processing) to analyze the emotional tone of your writing. Our model is trained on a diverse dataset of mental health related texts to identify patterns associated with Stress, Anxiety, Depression, and more.
